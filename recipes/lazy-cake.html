Lazy cake

Did you know?

It is currently unknown whether lazy cake originated from Eastern Europe or Middle East.

Lazy cake is one of my favourite recipes. It is delicious, doesn't require a lot of time or culinary skills. On top of that it only requires four simple ingredients!

Here they are:
  • Cocoa powder
  • Melted unsalted butter
  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • Biscuits

The steps:
  1. Stir together melted butter, cocoa powder and condensed milk until a smooth chocolate sauce forms.
  2. Crush your biscuits. Be careful not to crush them too finely, you want to keep the chunks large. Afterwards fold them with the chocolate sauceto combine.
  3. Freeze the mixture for two to three hours.

And thats it, the dish is done!
